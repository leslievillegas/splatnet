Squib Thoughts

Final Desperation

While the user’s team is in the "danger status" user would get a huge boost to their ink recovery, intensify action, special rate, and a power that's hidden. The buffs would slowly wear off as the user’s team gets out of the danger zone. (Headgear ability only)

Squib Thoughts

Raging Respawns

Makes the user respawn and super jump faster on each death by another player. While having the ability equipped you could mash the buttons to jump and respawn even faster. (Possibly a shoe only ability)

Squib Thoughts

Moral Support

When the user is near their allies both the user and the allies nearby would slowly gain special automatically. (Clothing only ability)

Squib Thoughts

Gamer Vision

Allows the user to see the exact hp of everyone and the damage dealt to them as well. Now you can see if the game is actually registering those hits. (Headgear only ability)

Squib Thoughts

Inklust

For every kill the user gets they gain a run speed and swim speed buff that lasts for 10 seconds. It resets on a kill by the user. They'll lose a lot of special meter gauge if they die though. (Headgear only ability)

More About Abilities

On top of choosing you favorite pieces of gear, you can choose your favorite abilities to add to them. Each ability is different and help you in battle.
