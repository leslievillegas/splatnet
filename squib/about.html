About Us

Learn more about us and our favorite weapons.

Verdigo
Favorite Weapon:
Big Swig because it has a a wide range coverage in its attacks and has a good overall movement speed

Pope
Favorite Weapon:
Nautilus because both the 47 and 79 have good kits that allow for different play styles. Has good range as well as a charge hold.

Sparky
Favorite Weapon:
Reef-Lux Deco because it's best when base rushing. The splashwall protects oneself from enemy fire and the reefslider can get you away from the base for a quick entrance or easy escape.

Booyah
Favorite Weapon:
Hydra because you can hide behind walls while healing up and kill up to 4 people at once. Booyah bomb allows for panic escape if someone comes close range.

Nival Cat
Favorite Weapon:
Flingza Roller because the sub ink mine allows for placement in an area the enemies might frequent. When it goes off, your teammates can track the enemy and easily target them. Flingza has fighting mechanics that act like a splat roller when swung horizontally but give you the range of a dynamo when swung vertically. The flingza covers short, long, and crowd control giving it versatility.

Crashur
Favorite Weapon:
Tri- Stringer because it can do everything chargers can and more with faster charge time while still backlining. Has some weak kits but not as many weaknesses as a charger.

Who We Are

We are a group of six friends trying to write down and share our ideas for a game we all love. Our ideas are mostly thought up by our friend Verdigo and shared and edited between the rest of us. The ideas are then published here. We are planning to add character bios for each of us here soon.
