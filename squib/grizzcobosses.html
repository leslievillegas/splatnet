Grizzco Bosses

On this page, you will find ideas for bosses in Splatoon 3's salmon run mode. Our ideas consist of commander salmonid which are stronger versions of boss salmonids with unique gimmicks. Splat them, and you’ll be rewarded with a huge golden egg.

Squib Thoughts

1st Boss

Commander Fish Stick: Are buffed versions of a boss salmonid Make the tower more tall and difficult to scale. Taller so that most weapons are unable to reach it by looking up and difficult to climb by having it similar to Octostomp, where some parts of the wall are uninkable. The top third of the wall will be uninkable, so you have to use a squid surge Once you reach the top, the fish will start to spin differently to make it more difficult to shoot them all (Half will be up, half down, and switch every second, or they’ll move like a carousel). If you don’t get all of them fast enough they’ll turn to aim at you. Make their singing buff every nearby salmonid, making them move and act faster so Scrappers will vroom even faster and there will be more bombs thrown.

Squib Thoughts

2nd Boss

Commander salmonid would be a stronger Maws, one that swims faster, has a bigger circle, and for every chomp they fail, they get a little faster each time (It’ll reset if they get a splat). Since there is a bunch of people throwing bombs in the circle to get the kill, the Commander Maws gimmick is that if there’s more than one splat bomb in the circle, they would bounce off the bite. That means only one splat bomb would do the heavy damage, do it three times and you’ll take em out.

Squib Thoughts

3rd Boss

Mr Grizz's rematch: A secret boss in salmon run where mr grizz himself would come crashing in from orbit to see if you're worth keeping in the company he'll replace the king salmon that you were supposed to fight and he'll fight like his first phase in the final fight but he's picked a few more tricks like being MUCH more mobile and having access to the other kings moves and, he'll only become available to fight if someone on the team has beaten "After Alterna". Mr. Grizz would have several glowing weak points on his body that the players could attack to damage the him and attacking him elsewhere would do absolutely nothing to him. Upon defeating the CEO of Mammals, he would drop scraps of classified documents that can "trade" for grizz approved weapons and gear
