Grizzco Weapons

This page has Grizzco weapon ideas. Grizzco weapons are overpowered weapons in Splatoon 3's salmon run mode. They are created by Grizzco's founder, Mr. Grizz. Below are our Grizzco weapon ideas for the game.

Squib Thoughts

Grizzco Shooter

Would have a slow firing rate and even slower strafing speed but still, keep its ability to shred thousands in one shot. Shots are blocked and your character is thrown backwards with each shot.
