Sub Weapons

Sub weapons are secondary weapons that take a good amount of percentage from the ink tank. They are not meant to be used as primary weapons. If user is low on ink, sub weapon cannot be used. Below are our ideas for Splatoon.

Squib Thoughts

Flash Bomb

Bomb that flashes bright light when thrown. Takes 25% ink tank and if you get hit by it, it'll blind you, destroy your ears, and take 20 damage. If you have sub power up, the range increases as well as the torture of your eyes and ears.
