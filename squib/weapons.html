Squib Thoughts

Squidsplitter

Sword idea: Sharged slashes would make a shockwave that'll travel on the ground in a straight line. Your character would also swing downwards while performing the attack. The weapon itself is huge compared to the average inkling which might give it more range than the chainsaw sword at the cost of overall speed. Paired with splashdown for close-quarter chaos. If the charged slash is done in the air, the user would quickly fall to the ground the preform a shockwave attack as if they were always on the ground

Squib Thoughts

Love Stringer

Valentine-themed bow. It could have the effect of turning your enemies against each-other temporarily tap shot makes it so they can't attack you, and a fully charged shot makes them attack their allies uncontrollably. Those who are under the Love Bow's effects would have hearts floating above their head and their ink color changed to the color of the bow. A weapon you can use with or against your friends.

Squib Thoughts

Fast Brella

A quick firing brella that gets stronger for every perfect block which would have a 1-second window to perform it. The shield itself could be smaller than the undercover and thus wouldn't be able to block much. It could be based on a pocket umbrella (the kind that launches outward when you press the button on it).
